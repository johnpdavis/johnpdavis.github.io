Fourpoint Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on John Davis' website and Fourpoint for iPhone, iPad and any other devices and platforms.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Through using Fourpoint you enter information about your courses across multiple semesters and years of school.

The following information is synced between your devices using Apple’s CloudKit technology. This information is not shared with me, the developer.

GITHUB

This website is hosted by GITHUB. They have access to some basic technical information to perform this role, such as your IP address.

APPLE

Crash reports, logs, and rudimentary usage information are collected by Apple and provided to us in order to fix problems that result in an application crash. This is done with your consent by the operating system

The information entered into the app is synced between your devices using Apple’s CloudKit technology.

ADS AND ANALYTICS

This application is supported by Google Admob.

Google may use the advertising ID from the device on which the ad is serving to generate interests and demographics (for example, ‘sports enthusiasts’). Interests, demographics, and other data may be used to serve better targeted ads to the user.

INFORMATION USAGE

We use the information we collect to operate and improve our website, apps, and customer support.

SECURITY

The information stored in Fourpoint is not further encrypted than standard iOS device encryption. Information in fourpoint is sandboxed from other applications and not made available via the file system.

ACCESSING, CHANGING, OR DELETING INFORMATION

You may delete information from Fourpoint inbox by deleting courses, semesters, and schools from within the app on an individual basis. Note: deleting data from one device, will delete it from all.

CALIFORNIA ONLINE PRIVACY PROTECTION ACT COMPLIANCE

We comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

CHILDREN’S ONLINE PRIVACY PROTECTION ACT COMPLIANCE

We never collect or maintain information from our website or app from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website or app is structured to attract anyone under 13.

YOUR CONSENT

By using our site or apps, you consent to our privacy policy.

CONTACTING US

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, you may email fourpointapp@gmail.com.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page. Summary of changes so far: January 31, 2020: First published. September 22, 2020: COPPA Language clarified. Removed errant references to vantage point. Ported to new site.
