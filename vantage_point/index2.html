Vantage Point

Vantage Point creates galleries from photo albums and directories, giving creative collections a fast mosaic view without forcing them into a new file structure.

Privacy Policy

Birds Eye Viewing

Feed Vantage Point a folder or album and it arranges a gallery into a mosaic layout that honors image proportions.

Your images and photos remain in their original location, so there's no extra proprietary data to worry about.

Organizing

Add pictures through the iOS share sheet, or drag and drop. Add them to your folders and albums by moving them to galleries.

Showcasing

AirPlay Mirror to a compatible device and browse a gallery on the big screen.

Use iPhone or iPad UI as a remote control.

More

  • Supports galleries from Apple Photos and Files / Finder
  • Keeps files and photos in their original locations.
  • Contains actions for shortcuts and Siri to add images to galleries and inbox
  • Drag and drop images, or use context menus to get organized
  • Provides home screen widgets to display folders as mini mosaics
  • View galleries on Apple TV with AirPlay Mirroring.
  • Supports iOS, iPadOS, macOS, and visionOS
